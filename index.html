About YLP Nigeria

About YLP

YLP was formed to give the youth of Nigeria between the age of 18-45 years the much needed opportunity to participate and engage in the democratic processes of governance through a non-partisan platform.

YLP Mission

The mission of YLP Nigeria is to build the future leaders and engage the youth in democratic processes of governance through a non-partisan platform.
